## 7 (Optional) — Invite connections to follow the new company page

Once you've re-linked Experience (step 5), open <https://www.linkedin.com/company/121203974/admin/dashboard/> → **Invitations** sidebar → **Invite connections**.

LinkedIn lets every page admin invite ~250 connections per month for free. Send invitations in batches:
- Round 1: existing patients + family + friends
- Round 2: professional contacts (pediatricians, OBs, midwives, PTs)
- Round 3: chiropractic colleagues, anyone else relevant

This gets you from 0 → couple hundred page followers fast, which both unlocks LinkedIn's algorithm boosts (pages with 100+ followers get more reach) and gives the company page social proof.
