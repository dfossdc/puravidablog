## What Is the Sacro-Occipital Technique?

The Sacro-Occipital Technique (SOT) is a comprehensive chiropractic method developed by Major Bertrand DeJarnette in the 1920s. It focuses on the relationship between the sacrum (the bone at the base of your spine) and the occiput (the base of your skull) — two areas that form the boundaries of your central nervous system.

SOT uses **gentle, low-force adjustments**, specialized wedge-shaped blocks, and cranial techniques to restore balance and optimize nervous system function. It's one of the safest and most thorough chiropractic methods available.
