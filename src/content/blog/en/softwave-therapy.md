## What Is SoftWave Therapy?

SoftWave Therapy uses broadband, low-intensity unfocused shockwaves to stimulate cellular repair at the tissue level. Unlike traditional focused shockwave devices, SoftWave's patented electrohydraulic technology disperses energy over a wide treatment area, reaching deeper tissue layers while remaining comfortable for the patient.

The waves trigger a biological cascade: stem cells are recruited to the damaged area, inflammation is modulated, blood vessel formation is stimulated, and cellular regeneration begins. The result is genuine tissue healing — not just temporary symptom relief.
