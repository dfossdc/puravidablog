# Auto Injury Chiropractor in San Antonio: What to Do After a Car Accident

A car accident can be one of the most disorienting experiences of your life. Even a relatively minor collision can leave your body in a state of physical trauma that may not be fully apparent for hours — or even days — afterward. If you've recently been in an accident in San Antonio, knowing your next steps could make the difference between a full recovery and chronic, lingering pain.

## Why Symptoms Are Often Delayed After an Accident

In the immediate aftermath of a collision, your body floods with adrenaline and other stress hormones. This natural response can mask pain and reduce your perception of injury at the scene. Many accident victims feel fine immediately after the crash, only to wake up the next morning with severe neck stiffness, back pain, headaches, or dizziness.

These delayed symptoms are most commonly the result of whiplash — the rapid back-and-forth snapping of the neck that occurs during a collision — along with soft tissue injuries to muscles, ligaments, and spinal discs. Without prompt evaluation and treatment, these injuries can develop into chronic conditions that limit your quality of life for months or years.
