# Pediatric & Prenatal Chiropractic in San Antonio: Safe Care for Every Stage

A healthy spine and nervous system are foundational to wellbeing at every age — from the first months of pregnancy through childhood and beyond. ## Prenatal Chiropractic Care: Supporting Mom and Baby

Pregnancy is one of the most physically demanding experiences a body can undergo. As a growing baby shifts a mother's center of gravity, the pelvis, sacrum, and lumbar spine face increasing stress. Hormonal changes also loosen ligaments throughout the body, which — while necessary for birth — can create instability and discomfort.

### Benefits of Chiropractic During Pregnancy

Prenatal chiropractic care at [Pura Vida Chiropractic](/en/services/pediatric-prenatal) offers San Antonio mothers a safe, drug-free way to manage the physical demands of pregnancy:

- **Relief from low back and pelvic pain** — one of the most common complaints during pregnancy
- **Reduced round ligament pain** and hip discomfort
- **Improved pelvic balance** — supporting optimal baby positioning for birth
- **Reduced likelihood of posterior or breech presentation** through Webster Technique
- **Shorter labor times** — studies suggest well-aligned pelves support more efficient labor
- **Management of sciatica** and leg pain during pregnancy

Dr. Dan Foss is trained in the **Webster Technique**, a specific chiropractic analysis and adjustment method recognized by the International Chiropractic Pediatric Association (ICPA) as a safe and effective protocol for pregnant patients. The technique addresses sacral misalignment and reduces tension in the uterine ligaments, creating a more balanced uterine environment.

### Is Chiropractic Safe During Pregnancy?

Yes — when performed by a trained chiropractor, prenatal chiropractic care is extremely safe. Dr. Foss uses gentle, modified techniques appropriate for each trimester, including specially designed cushions that allow pregnant patients to lie comfortably during treatment. No high-force manipulation is used.

## Pediatric Chiropractic: Building a Healthy Foundation

Many parents in San Antonio are surprised to learn that children can benefit enormously from chiropractic care. The birth process itself — even in ideal circumstances — places significant compressive forces on a newborn's spine and cranium. As children grow, falls, sports, heavy backpacks, and prolonged screen time introduce additional spinal stresses that, left uncorrected, can affect posture, nervous system function, and overall health.

### Common Conditions Helped by Pediatric Chiropractic

- Colic and digestive discomfort in infants
- Difficulty latching or feeding asymmetrically
- Ear infections and sinus congestion
- Sleep disturbances
- Growing pains
- Scoliosis screening and management
- Sports injuries and postural corrections in older children
- Headaches in school-age children

Pediatric adjustments performed by **Dr. Dan Foss** are extraordinarily gentle — the pressure used on an infant is no more than you would comfortably apply to your own closed eyelid. Children typically enjoy their adjustments and respond rapidly to care.
