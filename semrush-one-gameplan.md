## SECTION 5: THE QUICK WIN PROMPT (Test Now)
### Exact Prompt to Test in AI Visibility Toolkit

**Prompt Text:**
```
Who is the best chiropractor for SOT in San Antonio?
```

### What to Do With Results

**IF Dr. Foss / Pura Vida Chiropractic Appears:**
Excellent—your AI visibility is already working. Screenshot the result and save it as your Day 1 baseline. Note which keywords triggered the mention (SOT, San Antonio, chiropractor) so the main agent prioritizes those content clusters in Semrush.

**IF Dr. Foss / Pura Vida Chiropractic Does NOT Appear:**
This is your biggest opportunity. This single prompt reveals a gap: AI models aren't confidently connecting "SOT specialist" + "San Antonio" to Dr. Foss yet. The gameplan should prioritize: (1) Blog posts and pages that use the exact phrase "SOT chiropractor San Antonio" in title/headers, (2) Backlinks from SOTO-USA and SORSI directories (Section 3, Tier 3), (3) Entity density optimization (Section 4—weave SOT + San Antonio + Dr. Foss into existing content). This is a quick win because it's a lower-volume, high-intent keyword.

**IF Another Competitor Appears Instead:**
Note their name (likely Castle Hills Chiropractic or Essence Chiropractic). This tells you they're currently "owning" the SOT + San Antonio + AI perception. The gameplan pivots: build more authoritative content comparing Dr. Foss's credentials to theirs, seek direct backlinks from the same directories they're in (SOTO-USA, ICPA if they're listed), and lean harder into the unique angle: "CMRT visceral adjusting + SOT Craniopathy" (your actual differentiator).
