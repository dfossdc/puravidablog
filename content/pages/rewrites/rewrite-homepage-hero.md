## SECTION 2: THREE PILLARS INTRO BLOCK

**The Pura Vida Protocol — Three Systems, One Body**

True healing doesn't happen when you treat the symptom. It happens when you find the source. The Pura Vida Protocol addresses your health through the three interconnected systems that drive function throughout your entire body.

---

**Pillar 1 — The Spine & Pelvis (Sacro Occipital Technique)**

SOT is not a spinal manipulation technique — it is a complete system of whole-body analysis developed by Dr. Major Bertrand DeJarnette that classifies spinal dysfunction into three distinct categories and corrects it using gravity-assisted pelvic blocks rather than high-force thrusting. When the sacrum and occiput are in proper relationship, every structure in between — your lumbar spine, thoracic spine, discs, and nervous system — functions the way it was designed to.

*Conditions addressed: low back pain, sciatica, sacroiliac joint dysfunction, disc herniation, neck pain, numbness and tingling, postural imbalance, sports injuries, scoliosis*

---

**Pillar 2 — The Organs (CMRT — Chiropractic Manipulative Reflex Technique)**

Every organ in your body shares a nerve supply with a specific spinal segment. When an organ is stressed, it reflexively pulls that vertebra out of alignment — and when the vertebra is misaligned, it further stresses the organ. This is the viscero-somatic reflex, and it is the reason some spinal adjustments simply refuse to hold. CMRT breaks the cycle by treating the organ-spine connection directly, through Chapman's reflex points and specific organ manipulation techniques that are only available through Advanced SOT certification.

*Conditions addressed: GERD, acid reflux, PMS, menstrual cramps, menopausal symptoms, adrenal fatigue, constipation, IBS, gallbladder dysfunction, liver congestion, kidney stress, allergies, asthma, blood sugar regulation, fertility challenges, ileocecal valve dysfunction*

---

**Pillar 3 — The Cranium (SOT Craniopathy)**

Your skull is not a fixed bone. It is 22 individual bones that move in a rhythmic cycle six to twelve times per minute — a motion driven by the production and reabsorption of cerebrospinal fluid. That fluid bathes and nourishes your brain and spinal cord. When the cranial bones lose their normal motion, the consequences ripple through your entire nervous system. SOT Craniopathy restores proper movement to each of the 22 cranial bones with feather-light precision — and when the cranium moves correctly, adjustments throughout the rest of the body become far more stable and lasting.

*Conditions addressed: headaches, migraines, vertigo, tinnitus, TMJ dysfunction, ear infections in children, post-concussion syndrome, learning difficulties, plagiocephaly, sinus congestion, sleep disruption, cognitive fog, trigeminal neuralgia*
