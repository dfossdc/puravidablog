## Why Posture Matters More Than You Think

Your posture is not about how you look. It is about how your spine functions and how your nervous system operates.

**For every inch your head moves forward:**
- The stress on your cervical spine increases 10 pounds
- Your breathing capacity decreases
- Your spinal cord tension increases
- Your immune function decreases

**Poor posture causes:**
- Chronic neck and upper back pain
- Headaches and migraines
- Reduced breathing capacity and fatigue
- Digestive dysfunction
- Reduced confidence and mood
- Accelerated spine degeneration

**Poor posture develops from:**
- Desk work and computer use (the #1 cause)
- Phone use and "text neck"
- Poor sleeping positions
- Weakness in postural muscles
- Spinal misalignment forcing compensatory posture

Most people try to correct posture through willpower — sitting up straighter, pulling shoulders back. This fails because the underlying structural problem is not addressed. Your spine is misaligned, your postural muscles are weak, and no amount of willpower fixes these structural issues.
