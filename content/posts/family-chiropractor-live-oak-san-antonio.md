## Care for Every Life Stage

**Pregnancy and Postpartum**
Expecting mothers benefit from regular prenatal care to ease pregnancy discomfort and optimize fetal positioning. After delivery, we support postpartum recovery and rehabilitation.

**Newborns and Infants (0-12 months)**
Early chiropractic assessment addresses birth trauma and supports healthy development. Colic, reflux, ear infections, and feeding difficulties often resolve with gentle cranial adjusting.

**Toddlers and Young Children (1-5 years)**
As children grow, we support developmental milestones — crawling, walking, running. We address postural issues and prevent problems that might otherwise persist.

**School-Age Children (6-12 years)**
Heavy backpacks, screen time, and school desks create postural stress. Regular care maintains spinal health and supports focus, behavior, and learning.

**Teenagers (13-18 years)**
Teenagers face rapid growth, social stress, sports demands, and extensive screen time. Regular care prevents postural problems and supports nervous system function during these critical years.

**Young Adults (18-40 years)**
This is when many people ignore their bodies. Regular chiropractic care prevents accumulated damage from poor posture, sedentary work, and high stress.

**Midlife Adults (40-65 years)**
Many people feel the accumulated effects of decades of poor posture and stress. Regular care can reverse much of this and restore energy and mobility.

**Older Adults (65+ years)**
Chiropractic care helps aging parents maintain balance, mobility, and independence. We address arthritis, postural changes, and nervous system function to support pain-free aging.
