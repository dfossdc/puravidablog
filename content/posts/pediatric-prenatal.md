### Why Children Need Chiropractic Care

A child's spine faces stress from the very first moments of life. The birth process — especially in difficult deliveries involving forceps, vacuum extraction, or emergency C-sections — can create tension in the cervical spine and nervous system. As children grow, falls, sports, heavy backpacks, and hours on digital devices accumulate structural stress that can affect long-term health.

Early detection and correction of these spinal issues can prevent them from becoming chronic problems in adulthood. The nervous system controls and coordinates every function in a child's growing body — digestion, immune function, sleep, cognitive development, and more. When the spine is properly aligned and free from interference, the nervous system can work at peak efficiency.

### Pediatric Conditions Dr. Foss Treats

**Colic and Infant Irritability**
Many colicky babies have tension in the upper cervical spine and nervous system that interferes with digestion and sleep. Gentle chiropractic adjustments can relieve this tension, often providing surprisingly rapid relief.

**Chronic Ear Infections (Otitis Media)**
Dysfunction in the upper cervical spine can affect Eustachian tube drainage, creating the stagnant, moist environment where ear infections thrive. Numerous studies show that chiropractic care can significantly reduce the frequency of recurrent ear infections in children.

**Torticollis**
Torticollis — whether congenital or acquired — produces a head tilt or rotation that can affect visual, postural, and brain development. Dr. Foss has extensive experience treating torticollis with gentle, effective techniques.

**Scoliosis**
Early diagnosis and chiropractic treatment of scoliosis can slow or stop the progression of spinal curvature in children, potentially avoiding the need for bracing or surgery later in life.

**Youth Sports Injuries**
From ankle sprains to growth plate injuries, Dr. Foss provides comprehensive evaluation and treatment for young athletes, with a focus on complete healing before return to play.

**Growing Pains**
Many "growing pains" actually have a musculoskeletal component that responds well to chiropractic care, particularly when affecting the knees, hips, or back.

### How Pediatric Adjustments Are Different

Chiropractic adjustments for children are radically different from adult adjustments. Dr. Foss applies very gentle, controlled pressure — often comparable to the pressure you'd use pressing a tomato to check ripeness — to delicately mobilize joints and reduce nervous system tension.

### Benefits of Chiropractic Care During Pregnancy

Pregnancy creates significant changes in posture, center of gravity, and pelvic mechanics. Regular chiropractic care during pregnancy can:

- Relieve low back pain and pelvic discomfort
- Reduce tension in the ligaments supporting the uterus
- Improve hip and pelvic mobility to facilitate labor
- Support optimal fetal positioning for delivery
- Reduce labor time and complications

The **Webster Technique** is a specific sacral analysis and adjustment protocol designed to balance the pelvis and reduce uterine ligament tension. Dr. Foss is certified in this technique and has helped many mothers experience more comfortable pregnancies and smoother deliveries.
