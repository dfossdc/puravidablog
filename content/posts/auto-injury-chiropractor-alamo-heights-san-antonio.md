## The Hidden Injuries in Auto Accidents

Most people think auto injury is obvious — you feel pain immediately, so you go to the ER or urgent care. But here is what actually happens in a collision:

**Whiplash and Cervical Injury**
Your head and neck experience rapid acceleration and deceleration forces that tear small muscles, ligaments, and nerve fibers. You may feel fine for hours or even days — then stiffness, pain, and neurological symptoms appear. This is a sign that deep tissue damage has occurred.

**Spinal Misalignment**
The impact forces the vertebrae out of alignment. While the bones may not be broken, the ligaments that hold them in place are stretched or torn. This creates ongoing instability and nerve irritation.

**Soft Tissue Inflammation**
Muscles, tendons, and ligaments throughout your spine and pelvis swell, creating pain, stiffness, and reduced range of motion.

**Hidden Disc and Nerve Damage**
Not every disc injury shows up on X-rays immediately. Some disc bulges and herniations develop over days or weeks as inflammation increases.

Without proper chiropractic care, these injuries become chronic. The inflammation becomes scar tissue. The misalignment becomes permanent. The pain that started after your accident becomes a lasting problem.

## Common Auto Injury Patterns

- **Whiplash and neck pain** — the most common injury
- **Lower back strain** — from spinal compression at impact
- **Headaches and dizziness** — from cervical spine damage and nerve irritation
- **Shoulder and arm pain** — from brachial plexus irritation

- **Radiating pain down the arm or leg** — nerve root compression
