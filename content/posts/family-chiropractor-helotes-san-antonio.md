## Care for Every Life Stage

**Prenatal and Pregnancy**
Expectant mothers benefit from regular chiropractic care to ease pregnancy pain and support optimal baby positioning. We use gentle Webster Technique and SOT.

**Newborns and Infants (0-12 months)**
Early assessment and gentle cranial adjusting address birth trauma and support healthy development. Most pediatric complaints — colic, reflux, ear infections — respond beautifully to early chiropractic care.

**Children (1-12 years)**
As children grow, their spines need support through developmental milestones, school activities, and sports. We prevent postural problems before they become entrenched.

**Adolescents (13-18 years)**
Teenagers balance school stress, sports demands, screen time, and rapid body changes. Regular care maintains spinal health and supports nervous system function during these critical years.

**Young Adults (18-40 years)**
This is the "invincible years" when people often ignore their bodies. Regular chiropractic care prevents accumulated damage and keeps young adults performing at peak levels.

**Midlife (40-65 years)**
This is when many people feel the accumulated effects of years of poor posture, stress, and activity. Regular care can reverse much of this damage and restore energy and vitality.

**Older Adults (65+ years)**
Chiropractic care helps aging adults maintain mobility, balance, and independence. We address arthritis, posture, and nerve function to support pain-free aging.
