## What Is Class IV Laser Therapy?

Class IV laser therapy uses specific wavelengths of light to penetrate deep into tissue and stimulate healing at the cellular level. The laser energy boosts ATP production, reduces oxidative stress, and triggers anti-inflammatory and regenerative effects deep in muscles, joints, nerves, and spinal structures.

Unlike Class III (cold) lasers, which have limited penetration, Class IV lasers deliver enough energy to reach structures several centimeters below the skin surface — including the discs and nerve roots of the spine, deep rotator cuff tissue, and the interior of large joints like the knee and hip. Class IV laser therapy has FDA clearance for pain relief and adjunctive therapy in musculoskeletal conditions.
