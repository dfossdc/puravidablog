Pregnancy is one of the most physically demanding journeys a woman's body will ever go through — and you do not have to white-knuckle the back pain, sciatica, and pelvic discomfort that come with it. At **Pura Vida Chiropractic** in San Antonio, Dr. Dan Foss offers safe, gentle, and highly personalized **prenatal chiropractic care** designed to help expectant mothers throughout every trimester feel comfortable, aligned, and supported.

## Why San Antonio Moms Are Choosing Chiropractic During Pregnancy

As your body adapts to support a growing baby, your posture shifts, your center of gravity changes, and your pelvis and spine take on new demands. Prenatal chiropractic care addresses these changes at the root — restoring spinal and pelvic alignment, reducing nerve irritation, and supporting your body's natural ability to adapt.

Prenatal chiropractic care is drug-free, non-invasive, safe throughout all trimesters, and complementary to your OB or midwife's care.

## Common Conditions Treated During Pregnancy

- Low back pain and lumbar stiffness
- Sciatica during pregnancy
- Pelvic girdle pain and SI joint dysfunction
- Round ligament pain
- Pubic symphysis discomfort
- Neck pain and upper back tightness
- Rib discomfort from expanding ribcage
