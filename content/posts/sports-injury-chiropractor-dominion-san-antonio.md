## Common Sports Injuries We Treat

**Rotator Cuff Strains and Shoulder Impingement**
Throwing sports, swimming, and tennis create massive demands on the rotator cuff. We assess shoulder mechanics, decompress the joint, and rehabilitate the supporting muscles.

**Knee and Ankle Sprains**
Ligament sprains in the knee and ankle need more than rest. We use soft tissue therapy to accelerate healing, restore proprioception, and prevent chronic instability.

**Tennis and Golfer's Elbow**
Overuse injuries of the elbow are stubborn because we keep using the arm during recovery. We treat the underlying cause — muscular imbalance and joint restriction — not just the inflammation.

**Hamstring and Calf Strains**
Explosive athletes (sprinters, soccer players) blow out hamstrings and calves regularly. We accelerate healing and reduce the risk of re-injury through targeted soft tissue work.

**Lower Back Strain**
High-impact and rotational sports create lower back stress. We restore spinal alignment, release muscular tension, and teach movement patterns that keep your back safe.

**Hip and Glute Dysfunction**
Weak or dysfunctional hip and glute muscles are the root cause of many running injuries. We identify the weakness and rebuild strength and stability.

## FAQ

**Do I need imaging for my sports injury?**
Not always. If your pain is severe or the injury is major (complete ligament tear, fracture), imaging is important. Most sports injuries respond to care without imaging.

**Can I train while recovering?**
Yes. In fact, smart training during recovery accelerates healing. We guide you on what movements are safe and beneficial, and which to avoid.

**Will I need surgery?**
Most sports injuries respond beautifully to conservative chiropractic care. Surgery is rarely necessary. We assess carefully and refer to orthopedics only when conservative care is not working.

**How long until I can return to full competition?**
Mild injuries: 2-4 weeks. Moderate injuries: 4-8 weeks. Severe injuries: 8-12 weeks or longer. We tell you honestly and never clear you before you are truly ready.

**Do you work with athletic trainers or coaches?**
Absolutely. We communicate freely with your athletic trainers, coaches, and personal trainers to ensure coordinated care.
