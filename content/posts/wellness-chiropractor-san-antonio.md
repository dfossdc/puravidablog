## What Is Wellness Chiropractic Care?

Wellness chiropractic care is regular, proactive care designed to maintain optimal spinal alignment, nervous system function, and overall health — even when you are not in pain. Your nervous system controls every function in your body. When the spine is properly aligned and the nervous system is free from interference, every system in the body functions better.

Pain is the last symptom to appear when something goes wrong with the body — and the first to disappear. The absence of pain does not mean your spine and nervous system are functioning optimally. Think of wellness chiropractic the same way you think of visiting the dentist regularly: you don't wait for a severe cavity to get a cleaning. You go regularly to prevent problems before they start.
