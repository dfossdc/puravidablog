## The Wellness Philosophy

Chiropractic is built on a simple but powerful principle: your body is a self-healing organism. When your spine is properly aligned and your nervous system is free from interference, your body functions optimally.

Most people do not think about their spine until pain forces them to. By then, significant damage has accumulated. Vertebrae are misaligned. Discs are degenerating. Nerves are irritated. Scar tissue has formed.

Wellness care prevents this. By maintaining spinal alignment and nervous system function through regular chiropractic care, you prevent the damage that leads to pain, disease, and dysfunction.

## FAQ

**I feel fine. Why would I need wellness care?**
By the time you feel pain, significant spinal damage has often occurred. Wellness care prevents that damage from developing in the first place. Feeling fine now does not mean your spine is perfectly aligned.

**How often should I come in for wellness care?**
That depends on your lifestyle, work demands, stress level, and family health history. We recommend weekly or biweekly for the first month to assess your responsiveness, then move to monthly or quarterly maintenance.

**Will wellness care get expensive?**
Regular wellness care is far less expensive than treating chronic pain or disease. Most patients find wellness visits very affordable — often comparable to a gym membership.

**Does insurance cover wellness care?**
Some insurance plans do. We verify coverage at your first visit. Many patients choose to pay out of pocket for wellness care because the investment pays for itself in health and vitality.

**How long before I notice results?**
Most patients notice improved energy, better sleep, and improved mood within 2-4 weeks. Over months, posture improves, pain decreases, and vitality increases.

**Can I do wellness care indefinitely?**
Yes. Wellness care is a lifetime practice. Many of Dr. Foss's patients have been under wellness care for years or decades.
