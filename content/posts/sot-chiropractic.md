## What Is the Sacro-Occipital Technique?

The Sacro-Occipital Technique, or SOT, focuses on the relationship between the sacrum (the base of your spine) and the occiput (the base of your skull). When these two structures fall out of alignment, the effects ripple throughout your body — causing back pain, neck pain, headaches, and a wide range of other symptoms.

Dr. Foss holds one of the highest certifications available in SOT through SORSI — Advanced SOT Certification — making him one of a small number of chiropractors in San Antonio qualified to provide this level of care.

## How SOT Works

SOT uses wedge-shaped blocks placed under specific points of your pelvis to allow gravity itself to gently correct spinal and pelvic misalignment. This makes it one of the gentlest yet most effective chiropractic approaches available — ideal for patients of all ages, including children, pregnant women, and seniors.
