## What Pregnancy Changes in Your Body

Your spine and pelvis undergo dramatic shifts:

- **Center of gravity shifts forward** as your uterus grows, pulling your lumbar spine into excessive curvature
- **Relaxin loosens all ligaments** — not just in your pelvis, but everywhere — making you more mobile and more vulnerable to misalignment
- **Your pelvis widens and rotates** to accommodate the baby
- **Breast tissue increases**, pulling your upper back and shoulders forward
- **Your ribcage flares outward** to make room for your diaphragm

These changes are natural and necessary. But without chiropractic support, they often lock into painful patterns: lower back pain, sciatica, pelvic girdle pain, rib discomfort, and headaches become your constant companions.

Chiropractic care does not "treat" pregnancy. It supports it by keeping your spine and pelvis balanced through these natural adaptations.
