## Why Pregnancy Chiropractic Care Matters

Pregnancy is beautiful, but it is also physically demanding. As your baby grows, your body undergoes tremendous changes:

- Your center of gravity shifts forward
- Your pelvis widens and relaxes to prepare for birth
- Your spine curves more to accommodate your growing belly
- Your ligaments loosen under hormonal influence
- Your postural muscles work overtime

All of this creates lower back pain, pelvic pain, rib pain, and the general discomfort that so many pregnant women experience.

More importantly, spinal misalignment and pelvic dysfunction can affect fetal positioning. A baby who is not in the optimal head-down position has a harder time descending through the pelvis during labor, increasing the risk of complications and emergency cesarean delivery.

Chiropractic care addresses both the mother's comfort and the baby's positioning.

## The Webster Technique

The Webster Technique is a specific chiropractic approach designed specifically for pregnancy. Dr. Foss uses:

**Sacral Adjusting**
We adjust your sacrum (the base of your spine) to restore proper alignment and pelvic balance. This reduces pelvic tension that can interfere with fetal positioning.

**Ligament Release**
The round ligaments that support your uterus tighten as your belly grows. We release these ligaments to reduce tension and discomfort.

**Soft Tissue Release**
We release tight muscles and ligaments in your pelvis, abdomen, and lower back using gentle soft tissue techniques.

**Postural Support**
We teach you postures and movements that support proper fetal positioning and reduce strain on your spine and pelvis.

## Common Pregnancy Discomforts We Resolve

- **Lower back pain** — the most common pregnancy complaint
- **Pelvic pain** — pain in the pubic bone, sacroiliac joints, or throughout the pelvis
- **Rib pain** — baby kicks high and compresses your ribs
- **Sciatica** — nerve pain down your leg from pelvic pressure
- **Postural strain** — the cumulative effect of pregnancy posture changes
- **Sleep disruption** — pain and discomfort prevent restful sleep

## FAQ

**Is chiropractic care safe during pregnancy?**
Yes. When performed by a trained prenatal chiropractor, adjusting is very safe and beneficial throughout pregnancy.

**Can adjustments cause miscarriage?**
No. There is no evidence that chiropractic adjustments increase miscarriage risk. Properly performed adjustments are safe from the first trimester onward.

**What if I have already been diagnosed with low amniotic fluid or placental issues?**
We coordinate closely with your OB. In some cases, certain techniques are avoided. We communicate with your physician and adjust care accordingly.

**Can chiropractic help turn a breech baby?**
The Webster Technique is designed to optimize fetal positioning, which often allows breech babies to turn naturally. It is not a direct turning technique. Success rates are high, but not guaranteed.

**How soon after delivery can I return to care?**
We can resume care immediately after vaginal delivery if you feel ready. After cesarean, we wait 1-2 weeks to allow the incision to begin healing, then start with gentle techniques.

**Do you continue care postpartum?**
Many mothers choose postpartum care for recovery support, core rehabilitation, and to address any lingering pain. This accelerates your return to pre-pregnancy function.
