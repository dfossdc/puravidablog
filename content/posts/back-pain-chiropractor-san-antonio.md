## Why Back Pain Keeps Coming Back

Most back pain develops over time as a result of poor posture, repetitive strain, old injuries that were never fully resolved, or structural imbalances in the spine and pelvis. When the underlying cause is not addressed, the pain keeps returning — sometimes getting worse with each episode.

This is why the standard approach of rest and anti-inflammatory medications often fails. It manages the symptom but ignores the cause. Chiropractic care works differently. By restoring proper alignment, joint mobility, and nervous system function, chiropractic gets to the source of the problem.

## Common Causes of Back Pain Dr. Foss Treats

- **Lumbar muscle strain and ligament sprain** — the most common cause of acute low back pain
- **[Disc herniation and bulging discs](/en/blog/herniated-disc-chiropractor-san-antonio)** — when disc material presses on spinal nerves
- **Degenerative disc disease** — wear and tear that reduces disc height and causes instability
- **Sacroiliac (SI) joint dysfunction** — misalignment of the joint between the spine and pelvis
- **Spinal stenosis** — narrowing of the spinal canal that compresses nerves
- **[Scoliosis](/en/blog/scoliosis-chiropractor-san-antonio)** — abnormal lateral curvature of the spine
- **Postural back pain** — chronic strain from prolonged sitting or desk work
- **Facet joint syndrome** — inflammation and degeneration of the small joints along the spine
