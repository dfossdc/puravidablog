Membership tiers run $480-$1,800/year. The mid-tier ($895/yr) gets you a directory listing + member directory link + 2 chamber events. Lower marginal cost than a single GBP ad campaign and the link is permanent.

## What NOT to do

- **Don't buy backlinks.** Many of the Tier 5 domains in the report are paid-link-spam services that competitors evidently bought. Those will tank rankings under Google's link spam policies, and Castle Hills having one doesn't make it safe for Pura Vida.
- **Don't write guest posts on irrelevant blogs.** Nashvillefitmagazine.com, onebodyldn.com — irrelevant local context = low value + guilt-by-association risk.
- **Don't link-exchange with other SA chiros.** Reciprocal links between competitors signal coordinated link-building to Google.
