## TL;DR — Why safamily is winning

**Pura Vida's SEO fundamentals are actually BETTER than safamily's. The visibility gap is driven by 3 things, in this order:**

1. **Exact-match brand**: "San Antonio Family Chiropractic" literally contains the head keyword "San Antonio chiropractic". Google interprets this as the canonical match for the query. Pura Vida's brand doesn't include "San Antonio".
2. **Concentrated authority on one homepage**: 87.7% of safamily's traffic comes from just 2 URLs (homepage + www variant) ranking on 23+76 keywords. Pura Vida spreads authority across 645+ pages — averaging **0.24 traffic/page** vs safamily's **107 traffic/page**.
3. **Domain age + accumulated Authority Score (23 vs 9)**: safamily has been around long enough to build trust. Pura Vida is recovering from the Doctible-era traffic crash (-64.93% organic) and still rebuilding.

**The bullet that hits hardest**: On the 9 highest-volume "San Antonio chiropractor" keywords (combined ~6,000 monthly searches), safamily ranks #1-#8 while Pura Vida ranks #21-#40. That's 20-30 position gaps that translate to ~99% of head-term traffic going to safamily.

## The Gameplan to Beat Everyone

### Tier 1: Highest Impact (next 30 days)

#### 1. Build the homepage into the head-term pillar
Pura Vida's homepage has 5,572 words — close to safamily's 6,213 — but the topical focus is split across:
- SOT specialty
- Bilingual/Spanish community
- CMRT organ adjusting
- Cranial work
- Family practice

**Action**: Add a dedicated "Chiropractor in San Antonio TX" section to the homepage with H2s like:
- "Looking for a chiropractor in San Antonio?"
- "Why Pura Vida is San Antonio's choice for chiropractic care"
- "Serving every San Antonio neighborhood"
- "The best chiropractor in San Antonio is one who treats the root cause"

These don't replace the SOT/bilingual content — they ADD a layer of head-term targeting. Goal: increase keyword density for "chiropractor san antonio" / "chiropractic san antonio" without keyword stuffing.

#### 2. Optimize the on-page title pattern to win the head term
Current homepage title: "Pura Vida Chiropractic | Top Chiropractor in San Antonio TX" (60 chars — fine)

**Action**: Test variations like:
- "Chiropractor in San Antonio TX | Pura Vida Chiropractic — SOT Specialist"
- "Pura Vida Chiropractic — Top-Rated Chiropractor in San Antonio TX"

Lead with the keyword, not the brand. safamily does this on every page.

#### 3. Build a "San Antonio Chiropractor" hub page
Currently `/en/chiropractor-near-me-san-antonio` exists but isn't on the homepage radar. Position it as the canonical "What you need to know about chiropractic care in San Antonio" page. 2,000+ words. Include:
- Why see a SA chiropractor
- What types of conditions SA chiropractors treat
- How to choose a SA chiropractor (criteria checklist)
- Cost / insurance / what to expect on first visit
- Pura Vida's role as the only Advanced SOT chiropractor in SA

Internal-link this from the homepage, footer, every service page, every condition page.

#### 4. Win local pack rankings
safamily participates in 19.3% of "Other SERP Features" (= Local Pack mostly). Pura Vida should match this.

**Action items for Dan (not code)**:
- **Audit and complete Google Business Profile** — verify the profile has 100+ photos, weekly Posts, all categories selected (Chiropractor, Holistic medicine practitioner, Sports medicine clinic, Family practice physician)
- **Drive review velocity**: target 4-8 new Google reviews/month. Pura Vida has 159 reviews at 4.8★ — safamily probably has 200-400 reviews. Reviews drive local pack rankings as much as backlinks.
- **GBP Q&A**: seed 10-15 questions and answers on the GBP itself (the AI uses these for AI Overview)

### Tier 2: Medium-Term (60-90 days)

#### 5. Consolidate thin pages
Pura Vida has 645+ pages averaging 0.24 traffic/page. Some of these dilute authority.

**Action**: Audit which condition pages are getting <1 visit/month. Consider:

This is COUNTER-INTUITIVE — Pura Vida's existing strategy added MORE pages. But the data shows safamily wins with FEWER pages of higher individual quality.

**Action**:

  - "quiropractico cerca de mi san antonio"

  - "quiropractico bilingue san antonio"
  - "doctor de columna san antonio"

#### 7. Aggressive backlink acquisition with anchor text discipline
Pura Vida already has +10% growth in ref domains over the past month. Sustain and target this.

**Action**:
- For every new backlink request, ask for anchor text variations of "Pura Vida Chiropractic San Antonio" (NOT just "Pura Vida"). Right now Google has to do too much work to associate "Pura Vida" with "chiropractor San Antonio".
- Target 5-10 new backlinks/month from:
  - San Antonio chamber of commerce
  - Local news sites (San Antonio Current, MySA, Express News)

### Tier 3: Strategic (6-12 months)

#### 8. AI search dominance
safamily has 22 AI mentions (Pura Vida has 3). They're winning ChatGPT with 10 mentions and AI Mode with 11.

But on AI-content optimization, **Pura Vida is positioned to leap ahead** because:
- Pura Vida has FAQPage JSON-LD on 200+ pages (safamily has none)
- Pura Vida has comprehensive llms.txt

The deficit is just RECOGNITION — AI hasn't crawled the FAQ-rich pages yet to update its understanding. Within 1-3 months, AI search visibility should accelerate.

**Action**: Monitor AI Visibility weekly. If it doesn't double in 90 days, dig deeper into why AI isn't picking up the FAQ signal.

#### 9. Specialty content moat
safamily has zero presence on SOT/CMRT/cranial/Webster/pediatric — but these are LOW-VOLUME queries individually.

The opportunity: become the **definitive** information source on these niches. The 320-vol "chapman reflex points" where Pura Vida already ranks 4 — there are 100s of similar SOT/CMRT/cranial long-tail queries. Each is 50-500 vol but cumulatively they're significant.

**Action**: Build out 30-50 SOT-niche content pieces over 6 months. These will:
- Drive direct traffic from low-volume but high-intent queries
- Build topical authority that helps the head-term ranking
- Position Pura Vida as the SOT authority for chiropractic referrals from other DCs

### Tier 4: Risky but Considered

#### 10. Brand naming (longer term consideration)
The single biggest structural advantage safamily has is the "San Antonio" in their brand name. Pura Vida could:

- **A. Use "Pura Vida Chiropractic San Antonio" as the consistent everywhere-brand** (current). Push for backlink anchor text variations of this. Over time, Google associates the full brand with the location.
- **B. Acquire a redirect domain** like `bestchiropractorsanantonio.com` or `sanantoniochiropracticcare.com` that redirects to puravidasanantonio.com. This is gray-hat but sometimes works.
- **C. Don't rebrand.** Pura Vida is unique and memorable; rebranding would burn equity.

Recommend: Option A (anchor text discipline) + Option B (one supporting domain if affordable). Definitely not C alone (status quo) or full rebrand.
