## Tracking + follow-up

- Send each email Tuesday-Thursday morning (highest open rates).
- Don't send both on the same day — space by 48 hours.
- If no reply in 7 business days, send a one-line follow-up: *"Hi [name], following up on the story idea I sent on [date]. Happy to simplify or change angle if helpful — let me know what would be most useful for you."*
- Don't follow up more than twice. After that, wait a quarter and re-pitch with a fresh angle (e.g., a season-tied hook like "back-to-school backpack safety" in August).

## Why these specific angles

The "huesero" angle is unique to SA — no other major Texas metro has the same density of Spanish-speaking households who'd recognize the term. That makes it a story Houston/Dallas chiros can't pitch. Editors prize geographic specificity because it's harder to find.

The "23 years in SA" angle anchors you as a credible source — newsrooms get burned by transient practitioners pitching them; long-time local presence is a green flag.
